Canon Pixma IP8720 Setup

The Canon Pixma ip8720 is a compact and excellent printing machine that offers great convenience and superb picture quality. It is capable of printing photographs and high-quality business documents up to 13 x 19 inches. Also, it is an ideal printing machine for both home and office use. Moreover, due to its lightweight design, it is easy to carry. Along with this, the Canon Pixma ip8720 setup procedure is easy to execute. Thus, the users are suggested to go through the complete information provided here to complete the setup process.

The Canon Pixma ip8720 supports a wireless printing feature through which the users can immediately connect their devices and print from anywhere in the home or workplace. Thus, the user can print from their smartphone through various applications such as AirPrint, Google Cloud Print, and the Canon print app.

Steps for Canon Pixma IP110 Setup

All users are recommended to read the entire information provided below and follow the instructions to complete the setup procedure.

Note: The users will have to access the official webpage, i.e., ij.start.cannon, to complete the setup procedure.

Turn “ON” Printer

  • In the beginning, the users will have to turn “ON” the printer.
  • Note: The users are supposed to insert one end of the power cable into the power socket and the other end to the backside of the canon printer to turn it “ON.”
  • After this, the users will have to turn “ON” the canon printer’s operational panel.
  • Then tap on the “Power” button.
  • Now from there, the users will have to choose the printer’s language as per their requirement.
  • Note: Make use of the navigation keys to choose the preferred language of the canon printer.

Download Canon printer drivers

To avoid any issues with the Canon Pixma ip8720 setup procedure, all users are advised to download and install the most recent and updated Canon printer drivers. Therefore, the users are advised to scroll down and follow the instructions listed below.

  • Initially, open the web browser.
  • Then, the users are advised to visit the official website, i.e., ij.start.canon.
  • After this, on the official website, click on the “Setup” option.
  • Now the users are recommended to look for the canon printer model from the given list or type the printer name in the required field.
  • After this, the users are suggested to click on the “Go” option.
  • Then the users will have to select the region.
  • After that, the canon Pixma ip8720 setup window will open.
  • After that, the users will have to click on the “Download” button on that window.
  • Now, the “.exe” file will start downloading on the Windows system.
  • Note: The “.dmg” file will download on the Mac operating system.
  • Note: Wait and let the download procedure complete.
  • Now, go through the on-screen instructions to carefully complete the canon printer drivers download and installation process. 

Install canon printer cartridge

Users must wait for the print carriage holder to become steady before proceeding. Therefore, once the carriage gets stable, follow the steps outlined below to install the Canon printer cartridge.

  • Carefully remove the tape from the canon printer cartridge.
  • Note: Ensure not to touch the contact on the canon printer cartridges.
  • After this, the users will have to insert the black cartridge in the left slot and the color cartridge in the right slot.
  • Note: The users will have to locate the canon printer cartridge with an upward angle in the slot.
  • Note: Now, the users are suggested to lock it gently after placing the printer cartridge.
  • After that, close the printer’s output cover.

Load the paper tray

  • Gently take out the paper tray, and stop after hearing a click sound.
  • Now the users are suggested to smoothly slide the purple paper guide and move it to the right side.
  • After that, carefully keep the paper in the paper tray.
  • Note: The users are advised to place the paper within the maximum paper limit. 
  • After that, carefully adjust the paper guide from the paper edges to fix it.
  • Note: Check and ensure that there should not be any bent paper because the paper jam issue will occur.
  • Once the user places the paper, they are advised to slide the paper tray into the printer.
  • After that, set the paper settings according to the printer display’s requirement.

So after performing the guidelines provided above, the canon Pixma ip8720 setup procedure gets complete. If, due to certain issues, any user fails to perform the setup procedure, they are recommended to take help from the canon customer service team. The experts are there to guide the users with the canon pixma setup procedure.
