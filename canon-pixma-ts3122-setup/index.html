    • Locate your Canon PIXMA TS3122 on a dust-free surface near your computer system.

    • Make sure the power cable’s other end is plugged into a wall outlet.
    • Turn On the power-up button and set preferences on the Canon TS3122 display.
    • Load enough white papers on the input paper tray from the printer’s rear.
    • Install ink cartridges.
    • Install canon ts3122 setup using the installation CD.
    • Test printing your documents.

    Canon PIXMA TS3122 Wireless Connection

    Canon printer’s Wireless connection is one of the most commonly used and easiest methods to connect your printer and operating system to established printing features. So here we’ve also recommended instructions to build the wireless connection for your Canon PIXMA TS3122 printer, see below; 

    Canon TS3122 Wireless Setup For Windows –

    • Make sure the canon ts3122 setup is downloaded.
    • Initially, enable the Easy Wireless Connect option on your PIXMA printer.
    • When the ON lamp starts flashing, press the Stop button.
    • Now, press and hold the Direct button.  
    • Once the Wi-Fi connection establishes, the Wi-Fi lamp will start blinking.
    • Tap the Start Setup button and choose a region.
    • Click Yes in the License Agreement window.
    • Hit Next and select the Method of Connection like Wireless connection.
    • Press WPS on your Wireless Router.
    • Enter key in the Network Key and wait for network setup and printhead alignment.
    • On the next screen, choose the list of additional software you want to install.
    • Your setup is complete once the software installation is over.

    Canon TS3122 Wireless Setup For Mac –

    • Here steps for your mac and Pixma TS3122 wireless setup using WPS are mentioned.
    • Thus, enable the Wi-Fi option on your Canon TS3122 printerand mac.
    • Select a wireless network and enter the security key.
    • Press the Network button and release it when you see the Wireless icon flash.
    • On your router, push the WPS button.
    • Once Pixma and router connect Wirelessly, printer lamps become steady.
    • Now download Canon TS3122 setup from ij.start.canon/ TS3122 and install it.
    • Your Pixma printer is ready to print with Mac.

    How to start printing with Canon Pixma Ts3122 printer?

    • Turn on your Pixma printer.
    • Open paper tray and fill plain papers.
    • Adjust the paper tray using Ts3122’s edge.
    • On your operating system, open the file you wish to print.
    • On your keyboard, press CTRL and P at once.
    • The print Dialog box on the system screen appears, so set the printing copies number.
    • Click OK.
    • Wait for your Pixma Ts3122 to give printed sheets.

    How to Troubleshoot Canon Pixma TS3122 issues?

    Canon Pixma TS3122 or even Canon’s any printer series offers support if you face any or issues. However, there are many possible ways that you can use to resolve and troubleshoot Pixma TS3122 printer issues.

    • If you find your Canon Pixma TS3122 is not printing a paper, check the FINE cartridge holder if jammed papers are there. Then keep the FINE cartridge holder side and pull jammed paper to remove all paper.
    • Make sure you clean your Canon printer cartridge from time to time.
    • If you are sure that the printer has no issue founded, instead there is a software issue, then uninstall the previous TS3122 software and reinstall the Canon TS3122 setup to start fresh printing steps.
    • In other cases, you can read online manuals on ij.start.canon or directly contact Canon support.
