Setup IJ Network Device Setup Utility 

(http://ij.start-canon)

Usually, http://ij.start-canon Utility Tool is automatically downloaded and installed while setting up “Driver” software on the computer. However, if it fails to instal on your system. Then, you can manually set it up once your printer is properly configured.

Prerequisites

  1. Procure LAN cable and Network Access Point or Router beforehand.
  2. Ensure that the computer and printer are not connected. 
  3. Besides, Disable the Firewall or Security Network on your computer before the Setup.
  4. Additionally, you must be logged in to your computer as an Administrator.

Setup Process

 

  1. First of all, open your preferred internet browser and go to the official manufacturer website.
  2. Then, you should select your “Canon product name”. In  other words, you should choose the Series and Model name from the drop-down box.
  3. In the Documents field, select the “Driver, Software and Firmware” option. 
  4. Thereafter, click the “Add” icon near the Downloads menu.
  5. Now, select your computer’s OS and its version.
  6. Next, you should locate and click the IJ Network Device Setup Utility (http://ij.start-canon) from the search list. 
  7. The following window will provide complete details of the http://ij.start-canon Utility Tool. Besides, it has the option to set your preferred language. Hence, review the details and select your preferred language. 
  8. Finally, click on the “Download” button to initiate the download process. 
  9. Thereafter, wait for the download to complete.
  10. Then, locate the downloaded Setup file and double-click on it to open the file.
  11. Now, click on “Run” and wait for the validation process to complete.
  12. Subsequently, follow on-screen instructions to complete the http://ij.start-canon Utility Tool setup process.
  13. Meanwhile, click “Yes” to permit installation when prompted. Besides, click on the checkbox for  “Agree to the Terms and Conditions of the Utility Software”. 
  14. At last, click on “Install”. 
  15. Then, wait for the process to complete and restart your computer.

With this, you can easily undertake various functions. For instance, change the administrator password, alter the status of your computer and printer, modify your network and wireless LAN settings, rectify connection issues and printer errors etc.

How to Access IJ Network Device Setup Utility 

(http://ij.start-canon)?

You can easily access http://ij.start-canon Utility Tool on your computer that empowers you with complete management control.

Windows 10

  • Click on “Start” button.
  • Then, select “All Apps”.
  • Now, click on “Canon utilities”.
  • Next, double-click on “IJ Network Device Setup Utility”.

Windows 8.1/ 8

  • Open the “Start Screen”.
  • Then, select “IJ Network Device Setup Utility”.
  • In case, if the Start Screen doesn’t display it. Then, select the “Search Charm” and search for  “IJ Network Device Setup Utility”.

Windows 7/ Vista

  • Click on “Start” button. 
  • Then, click on “All Programs”. 
  • Now, select “Canon Utilities”.
  • Thereafter, click on “IJ Network Device Setup Utility”.

Mac OS

  • Click “Go” from the “Finder” menu.
  • Then, select “Applications”. 
  • Next, double-click on “Canon Utilities” folder.
  • Now, select the “IJ Network Tool” folder. 
  • Thereafter, click on the “Canon IJ Network Tool” icon.
