How to Troubleshoot Canon Printer MX922 Printhead Errors? 

Canon MX922 print head error is a mechanical failure that you may face in daily use. This error will not let you print a page and occur repeatedly if you do not find a solution to resolve it. In this manual, you will get effective solutions that are easy-to-understand and simple to follow. 

Possible Reasons for Canon MX922 Print Head Error

  1. It may be possible that the printhead installed is not compatible or well-suited with your MX922 printer. In such scenarios, your printer will fail to communicate with the printhead when printing a page. 
  2. Printhead nozzles are choked. 
  3. This error may also occur if the cartridges you are using do not support your MX922 printer model. 
  4. While setting up the printer or inserting the ink cartridges, the printhead position mistakenly changed.
  5. If the cartridges you are using for printing pages are empty or suddenly stop functioning. 
  6. The error may get displayed, if any piece of paper gets stuck in the printhead area. 
  7. Sometimes, the Canon MX922 print head error occurs due to outdated drivers. 

Effective Methods to Resolve Canon MX922 Print Head Error

To quickly fix Canon MX922 print head error, have a look at the below-stated troubleshooting methods. 

Method 1: Reset the Canon MX922 Printer

  • The primary step is to turn off your printer. Now, press the ON button, and alongside this, press the Stop button. 
  • Afterwards, you have to release the Stop button for once and then press it again for five times. 
  • Now, release the ON button and your printer LCD screen will lit up. 
  • After a few seconds, you have to press the Power button to switch off your printer. This will reset your printer. 
  • Now, try to print a page. If the Canon MX922 Print head error still persists, carry out the next method. 

Method 2: Execute the Printhead Cleaning Process

Standard Cleaning
  • Power on your MX922 printer and load one or more pages into the printer tray. 
  • Press the Menu button given on the front panel of the printer. The printer display will lit up. 
  • Select the “Setup” option and then press OK. From the setup screen, you have to locate and select the “Maintenance” option. 
  • A list containing different options will come into view. Select the “Deep Cleaning” option and press OK. 
  • A pop-up box will get displayed asking for your confirmation to clean the printhead, select “Yes”. 
  • It will start cleaning your MX922 printhead. Once you see the “Pattern Print Confirmation” screen, select Yes and by following this, press OK. 
  • The print head nozzle check pattern will be printed on a page. When the completion message comes up on your printer screen, press OK.
  • Now, check the nozzle check pattern and print quality. If the issue is not fixed yet, step forward to the next method to resolve the Canon MX922 print head error. 
Take Out the Printhead and Clean it Manually
  • In the first place, open the top cover of your MX922 printer. Following this, open the scanner unit and the carriage will automatically adjust into its place. 
  • Now, you have to pull out the power from the backside of the printer. Doing so, your printer will be turned off immediately. Moving on, remove all the cartridges installed on your printer. 
  • Thereafter, take out the printhead carefully from the printer in order to start the cleaning process. 
  • Now, dip a cotton cloth in lukewarm water and gently clean all the ink patches that exist on the printhead. 
  • In addition, if any of the cartridges is empty, replace it with a new one. 
  • When you observe that the printhead is properly cleaned, let it dry for some time. Then, install the printhead again into its assigned place. 
  • Insert all the cartridges, close the scanner unit, and finally close the top cover of the printer. 
  • Now, attach the power cord to the printer, power it on, and try to print a page. This may resolve the Canon MX922 print head error. If not, proceed ahead and follow the next method. 

Method 3: Install the Latest Driver that Supports Your MX922 Printer

Printer driver is a software that sends print requests to your printer. So, if the software gets corrupted, then you may see the Canon MX922 print head error on your printer LCD screen. To fix the issue, uninstall the driver from your Mac or Windows operating system and then reinstall it. 

  • Run a compatible web browser and move ahead to canon.com/ijsetup
  • Input MX922 in the search bar, press Go and select the “Driver & Downloads” tab.
  • Make an appropriate selection for your Operating System and Language. Tap “Select” and by following this, click “Download”. 
  • Link your MX922 printer to the computer and accept the Installation agreements by clicking Yes. 
  • This will initiate the printer driver installation. Once it’s done, try to print a page by using the newly-installed printer driver.

How Do I Clear A Canon Printer Error?

Canon offers a variety of smart and highly innovative Printing Devices that are simply best-of-the-breed. These ingenious, user-centric gadgets are packed with unparalleled features. Just like any sophisticated machines, these Printers are prone to various technical glitches like Canon Printer Error. This is a very common issue that users encounter from time to time. 

Understand Canon Printer Error

While fixing Canon Printer Error is extremely important to ensure that it doesn’t cause permanent damage to your Printer. But, this is possible only if you diagnose the problem correctly.  When you get a Canon Printer Error, its Alarm Lamp starts flashing. Along with that, your Computer’s screen will provide a Support Code or an Error Number. In some cases, the Printer’s Power Lamp will also start flashing along with its Alarm Lamp. The status of these lamps along with the message gives you a clear understanding of your Printer’s problem. Based on the given Error Code, you should take appropriate action to clear this Canon Printer Error. 

Common Error Codes and Related Issue

  • Error 1003, 2100, 2101,1250, 1300, 1303, 1310, 4103, 2103, 2102, 1304 etc.: Paper Issues
  • Error 1687, 1401, 1403, 1485, 1486, 1487, 1702, 1703, 1704, 1705, 1712, 1713, 1714, 1715, 1890, 1686, 1684, 1682, 1688 etc.: Cartridge Issues
  • Error 5100, 5B02, 5B03, 5B04, 5B05, 5B12, 5B13, 5B14, 5B15, 5011, 5012, 5200, 5400, 6000, 6500, 6800, 6801, 6900, 6901, 6902, 6910, 6911, 6930, 6931, 6932, 6933, 6936, 6937, 6938, 6940, 6941, 6942, 6943, 6944, 6945, 6946 etc.: Printer Errors

Fix Canon Printer Error

As explained above, you must perform troubleshooting steps according to the given Canon Printer Error Code. Given below are some of the most common troubleshooting steps that can easily fix this issue. Before performing the troubleshooting steps, you must check certain basic things to ensure that your Printer functions properly. 

  • Ensure that your Printer is properly hooked to the Power Source and is turned on. 
  • Also, make sure it is properly configured to the Internet.
  • It must be securely connected to your Computing device.
  • Additionally, check for empty Paper Tray and Ink Cartridges. Make sure to fill them properly. 
  • Besides, clear any Paper Jams. 
  • Make sure that the Cover is not open. 

Troubleshooting Steps

Change Port Settings

  1. Press the Windows Logo Key along with “R” on your keyboard to get the “Run” Command window. 
  2. Here, you must type in “devmgmt.msc” in the given Search Bar. Then, hit the “Enter” key. 
  3. This will bring up its Device Manager. Go to its top menu. Then, click on “View”.
  4. Now, select “Show hidden devices”.
  5. Right-click on the “Ports (COM & LPT)”  tab. 
  6. Then, select “Properties”.
  7. Thereafter, click on “Port Settings” to open its Settings window. 
  8. After that, select “Use any interrupt assigned to the port”.
  9. Now, search for the “Enable legacy Plug and Play detection” option. Tick mark the corresponding Checkbox. Then, click “OK”.
  10. Finally, Restart your Computer. 

Turn Printer Online

  1. Click on the Windows “Start” button. 
  2. Look for the “Control Panel” tab on the following window. Click on it to go to your Computer’s Control Panel. 
  3. Select “Devices and Printers”.
  4. On the following window, a list of connected devices will appear. As such, select your Canon Printer. 
  5. Right-click on it. Make sure that the option for “Use Printer Offline” is not selected. However, if it shows selected. Then, click on it to deselect it. 
  6. Restart your Computer to clear Canon Printer Error. 

Reinstall Canon Printer Driver

  1. Go to the Control Panel of your Computer. 
  2. Select “Devices and Printers”.
  3. Locate your Canon Printer. Then, right-click on it. 
  4. Now, click on the “Remove Device” tab.
  5. Restart your Computer.
  6. Thereafter, launch a Web Browser. Then, go to “canon.com/ijsetup”.
  7. Here, you must search for your Canon Printer Model. 
  8. After that, locate the available Printer Driver. Click on the corresponding “Download” tab. 
  9. Wait for the download to complete. Then, open the downloaded file. 
  10. Then, follow on-screen instructions to successfully install the Driver Software. 
  11. Finally, Restart your Computer to fix the Canon Printer Error. 

Reset Print Spooler Service

  1. Press the Windows Logo Key along with “R” on your keyboard to get the “Run” Command window. 
  2. Here, you must type in “services.msc” in the given Search Bar. Then, hit the “Enter” key. 
  3. This will bring up a Search Result window. Go to its right-hand panel. Then, select “Print Spooler”.
  4. Right-click on it. Thereafter, opt for “Properties”.
  5. Click on the “General” tab. 
  6. Here, you must click on the “Startup Type” drop-down box. Select “Automatic”.
  7. Click on “Apply”. Then, select “OK”.
  8. After that, close down this window. 
  9. Finally, Restart your Computer.

Additional Steps

  • Update your Computer’s OS 
  • Use Windows Repair Tool to fix PC issues
  • Restore your Internet Connection

How to Fix Canon Printer Ink Error?

Canon Printer Ink Error displays when the ink level is low or empty and unable to print a document. You have the option to ignore this message by simply clicking on the OK button. Also, you can perform the basic reset process. For that, press your printer’s restart button or Color Start button for a period of at least 15 seconds. However, this article shows you some easy and advanced methods for resolving Canon Printer Ink Error. 

Method 1. Remove the Protective Strips or Covers of a new cartridge

If you have inserted the ink cartridges without removing its strips or covers, the printer will surely not recognize the ink cartridge. Hence, take out the cartridge, gently remove its protective strips and insert it again. Try to print a paper now. 

Method 2. Put the Canon printer ink cartridge in the correct slot

It is important that you have placed the cartridges into their assigned and specified slots. If the black ink cartridge is placed in the colored slot, then the printer will obviously fail to recognize the cartridge. Also, check that you are using the correct cartridges for your canon printer. For this, you can check the manual of your printer. 

Method 3. Remove the cartridge and clean the electrical contacts

If the printer still does not identify and recognize the cartridge, take out the Canon printer ink cartridge from the printer. Gently wipe the contact chip by using a clean towel or lint-free cloth. The contact chip helps in recognizing the ink cartridges. So make sure that the contact chip is properly clean and should not contain any dust particle. 

Method 4. Place the “Cartridge with print heads” properly

For cartridges with print heads, the cartridge is often placed flat or upside down for weeks or months. Due to this, the black or colored inks do not reach the print head and obstruct the new ink cartridge from printing. To resolve this issue, you have to remove the ink cartridge from its slot and place it on a towel with the print head facing down Also, you need to wait until and unless you see the ink flow. Once the ink reaches the print head, you should reinstall the ink cartridges and try to print. 

Method 5. Reset the Cartridge Ink Counter

Even after cleaning the electrical contact, if the issue does not resolve, you are supposed to reinstall the ink cartridges. For that, follow the given steps with accuracy. 

  • First of all, unplug the power cables of the printer. Detach the cable from the wall socket. Also, unplug all the USB and ethernet cables that are connected to your printer. 
  • Wait for at least 30 to 60 seconds. Then reconnect the power cable to the wall socket. 
  • Now, you are required to open the cartridge door. Press and hold the power button for a few seconds. 
  • While holding the power button, close the cartridge door. Then, release the button. 
  • This will reset both the printer and the ink cartridge.

Follow the below-given steps to resolve the Canon Printer Error 6000 completely. 

Causes Of Canon Printer Error 6000 

  • This issue occurs when the paper feed tray of printer prevents its opening when something gets blocked in it. It may be dust particles or paper pieces.
  • Error Code 6000 also may arise when the tray is not correctly fitted in the tray.
  • Also, this error may occur when we try to give a multiple paper print command at a single time.
  • Sometimes, it may also appear on your system, when the printer is surrounded by lots of documents.
  • The line feed is either scratched or smeared.
  • This error also arises, when any defect arises in the sensor unit or in-line-feed slit.

Solutions To Fix Canon Printer Error 6000 

Method 1. Procedure To Clean The Paper Feed Roller 

* For Windows 

  • Firstly, make sure that your printer is turned On. After that, remove if there is any paper from the rear tray.
  • After that, open the printer properties dialogue box using the procedure that may vary according to your Operating System.
    • First of all, press the windows and X key altogether from your keyboard.
    • Then, type “Control Panel” in the search box and press the enter key.
    • Next, select “Devices and Printers”.
    • As a result, devices and printer window will appear on your screen. 
    • Right-click on the listing of your printer and select “Printing Preferences” from the menu that appears.
  • Thereafter, click the maintenance tab and then “Roller Cleaning” tab.
  • Subsequently, you need to confirm the message and click on the “Ok” tab. 
  • As a result, the paper feed roller will rotate as they clean.
  • Next, you need to make sure that the paper feed roller stops rotating. After that, when the message appears, load three or more sheets of A-4 sized plain paper in portrait orientation in the rear tray. Here, you have to set the paper thickness lever to the right. 
  • Then, confirm the displayed message and click on the “Ok” button.
  • As a result, the paper that is loaded in the printer will feed through the printer and can be ejected. It is recommended not to perform any other operation while the paper feed roller is being cleaned. 
  • Finally, you need to confirm the displayed message and click on the “Ok” tab.

If still you are facing this problem, turn off the printer, unplug the power plug. After that, wipe the paper feed roller located on the right side inside the rear tray with the moistened cotton swab. Then, rotate the roller manually as you clean it. Do not touch the paper feed roller with your fingers, rotate it with the cotton swab.

* For Mac 

  • Firstly, make sure that your printer is turned On. After that, remove if there is any paper from the rear tray.
  • Then, open the paper output tray gently, and extend to open the output tray extension.
  • After that, open the Canon Printer IJ utility. 
  • For that, open “system preferences” and click “print and fax”. After this, select your printer’s name in Printers and click “Open Print Queue”.Now, click the Utility/ Printer setup icon. Then, select your printer name in the product list and click “maintenance”.
  • After that, you need to clean the paper feed roller. For that, make sure that cleaning is selected in the pop-up menu. Then, click on “Roller Cleaning”. Now, select the paper source to clean. Next, click on “Ok”. As a result, the paper feed roller will rotate as it is cleaned.
  • Next, you need to make sure that the paper feed roller stops rotating. After that, when the message appears, load three or more sheets of A-4 sized plain paper in portrait orientation in the rear tray. Here, you have to set the paper thickness lever to the right. 
  • Then, confirm the displayed message and click on the “Ok” button.
  • As a result, the paper that is loaded in the printer will feed through the printer and can be ejected. It is recommended not to perform any other operation while the paper feed roller is being cleaned. 
  • Finally, you need to confirm the displayed message and click on the “Ok” tab.

Method 2.

  • If the line feed is either scratched or smeared, you need to simply clean the LF or EJ slit film with a noncorrosive solution.

Method 3.

  • In case, if there is any stuck particles, debris or foreign object in the LF drive, remove the jammed object properly. 

Method 4. Procedure To Delete Print Jobs From The Print Queue 

  • Firstly, press the windows and R key altogether from your keyboard.
  • Thereafter, in the Run box, type “services.msc”. Click on the “Ok” tab.
  • As a result, the services window will appear. Here, you need to scroll down to the Print Spooler. Then, right-click on it and click on “Stop”.
  • Once again press the windows and R key altogether from your keyboard. 
  • After that, in the run box, type “spool” and click on “Ok”.
  • Now, type “%systemroot%\System32\spool\printers and then press the enter key.
  • Next, select all of the files by holding ctrl and a key altogether.
  • After that, press the “delete” key to delete all of the files.
  • Then, after deleting all the files, close the spool folder.
  • After this, go back to the services window. Now, right-click on the “Print Spooler Service” folder and click on the “Start” button.

Method 5.

  • Sometimes, even the defective component of the printer can affect the printer’s ability of the printer. So, to overcome this problem, it is important to replace the defective parts. The defective part of the printer may include LF and EJ slit film. It may also include a timing sensor unit, the paper feed roller unit, and the logic board. 

Method 6. Procedure To Restart Your Printer 

  • First of all, you need to unplug your printer device from the power switch. Then, leave as it is for a few seconds in the same pause mode.
  • After that, re-plug the power cord of your printer and again switch On it.
  • Thereafter, press the and hold the “Power” button for a few seconds. Next, you need to press the “Stop” button twice. 
  • Then, you need to repeat this procedure one or more times. 
  • Now, this time you need to release the “Power” button very slowly.
  • Lastly, you need to press the “Stop” button four times. 

Method 7.

  • In case, if your printer is surrounded with documents make sure that you secure the clear space in front of the tray before copying or printing. When the error message is displayed and in case if you find something is in front of the tray, remove it. After that, turn off the printer and then turn off the printer and back on again and the printer will be ready for printing.
