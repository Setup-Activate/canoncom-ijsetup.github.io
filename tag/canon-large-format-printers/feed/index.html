 The Best Canon Printers: Which Should You Buy?

Canon Printers offer high-quality photo and text printing, excellent scanning, user-friendly display, mobile printing, and much more. Here is a list of some canon printers that you can purchase according to your needs and requirements. 

➣ Canon PIXMA MG3620

The printer has wireless functions and can be connected with the computer and the router without using any cables. It has an advanced feature of duplex printing that enables the printer to print both sides of the pages by turning the page automatically. Also, you can easily print directly from your iPad, iPhone, Android mobile devices. Its other specifications are Google Cloud Print, Airprint, print space-saving design, 4800 x 1200 maximum color dpi, Near Field Communication printing, etc. 

➣ Canon MB5170 

Canon MB5170 has a large-capacity cartridge and you don’t need to replace the ink frequently. It has two Contact Image Sensors which enhances the quality of scanning for two-sided documents. The printer has an 8.89 cm touch-screen LCD display which gives you better visibility and thus reduces operational errors. The printer is capable enough to print 2500 pages with 50% of ink savings. With its high-density pigment ink, it prints sharp and clear documents. Hence, it is one of the best Canon printers. 

➣ Canon PIXMA G2010

The printer has a new operational panel design that provides a trouble-free experience while printing or scanning multiple documents. It is specially designed for high volume printing as it can print 7000 pages with its one-ink bottle. This is one of the recent Canon printers that offer high-quality and clear printing of images and documents. Also, the printer has a special tip on the ink bottles that reduces spillage while refilling the ink cartridge.

➣ Canon SELPHY CP1300

This high-quality printer can print the images directly from your camera. The printer features a unique film print overcoating that will not fade the ink color of the images for hundreds of years. It has a new Wifi Shuffle Print mode that can merge the images from 8 smart devices to make an exclusive collage on a single photo. It supports wireless printing with Airprint, Mopria, and has 8.12 cm LCD screen. Moreover, you can print in three different colors i.e Black White and Pink. 

➣ Canon imageCLASS MF445dw

It is basically designed for small and medium-sized businesses which also has an extra paper tray. It features Wifi direct hotspot which allows your printer to directly connect with the compatible mobile devices. It offers fast and duplex scanning for up to 70 images in black and 26 images in color per minute. It has a unique Application Library platform that you can execute by using the one-touch button from the home screen. Also, it has a 5” color touchscreen which provides user-friendly printing experience. 

➣ Canon PIXMA GM2070

This is one of the latest Canon printers which supports both the wireless and wired connection types. It has an auto-duplex printing feature through which you can easily print both the sides of a page. You can print 8300 pages with its High page yield ink bottle. It offers mobile and cloud printing so that you can print from smartphones by using the Canon Print Inkjet/Selphy application. Also, it features 2-way paper feeding and you can load paper in 2 directions i.e front cassette and rear tray.
